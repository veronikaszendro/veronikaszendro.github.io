VERONIKA SZENDRO


The artist, researcher, and gallery co-founder was born in 1990 in Szeged (HUN). She studied at the University of Pecs (HUN) and took place in an exchange program at Nazareth College (Rochester, NY, USA) for a semester in 2013. Currently, she attends the Doctoral School of Art at the University of PÃ©cs. In addition, she participated in a non-degree program at Yale Divinity School (New Haven, CT, USA) in 2022. She exhibited in group exhibitions in London (GB), Chicago (USA), Stockholm (SWE), Milan (IT), Saint Petersburg (RU), and Barcelona (SP). She is a video artist, but she also creates video installations because of her Master's studies as a sculptor. Her artworks and research focus on the connection between contemporary art and religions.
