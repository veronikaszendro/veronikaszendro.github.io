Cassandra Millenial

The art project aims to represent the thoughts of a virtual philosopher and poet, Cassandra Millenial. She creates poems about her life and uses the structures of famous philosophers to express her thoughts. The artworks illustrating her words are created using mixed media: hand-painted or drawn scenes are animated by video editing software.
